Roster Rule Check Info (Beta)

This tool provides general guidance and has not been tested on a wide audience and may contain inaccuracies. Always refer to the official EA document and your company's rostering policy for definitive interpretation. This tool flags potential deviations from standard roster rules; some may be permissible if mutually agreed.

The tool currently checks for the following potential roster deviations:

  • Early Starts [EA 14.8]: More than 3 consecutive starts before 06:00.
  • Early Starts (7-Day) [EA 14.8]: More than 3 early starts in any 7-day period.
  • Minimum RDOs [EA 14.11]: Fewer than 8 Rostered Days Off in a 28-day roster period (pro-rata for leave).
  • Weekend Off [EA 14.16]: No full Saturday/Sunday RDO block in a 28-day roster period.
  • Consecutive Duty Tours [EA 14.15]: Two tours of 6+ days not separated by at least 2 days off.
  • Sign-on/off Around Leave [EA 14.17]: Sign-off after 14:00 before, or sign-on before 12:00 after, a leave block of 7+ days.
  • Maximum Nights Away [EA 14.54]: Trips with more than 5 consecutive nights away from Home Base.
  • Training After Leave [EA 14.47]: Training/checking duties within 10 days of returning from 15+ days of leave.
  • Rest Before Standby [EA 14.50]: Less than 12 hours rest between a duty's sign-off and the start of a Standby.
  • Rest Periods (Home Base) [EA 14.48]: Less than 12 hours rest between duties at Home Base.
  • Short Rest Periods (Info): Less than 12 hours rest between duties away from Home Base.

The tool does NOT currently check for many other complex rules (e.g., FDP limits, specific buffer periods, etc.).
